  • about moosti

    Moosti is a simple time tracker with similar functions described on Pomodoro Technique®.

    features

    • Responsive Design

      Now you can use moosti on the device of your choice (from desktop computer monitors to mobile phones).

    • Notifications

      When the timer stops, we'll notify you with a desktop notification.

      To enable the notification, just accept on the first finished timer, and for sound, just enable on top-right of page.

    • Configuration

      You don't need to configure your sets everytime you enter on the site, moosti now save your last configuration, including buttons and audio states.

    • Colors

      Choose between different color palettes, just go over the menu at right.

    • Very objective

      To play with moosti just set the timers and go:

      - Focus: Set from 10 to 60 minutes;

      - Short Break: Set from 3 to 5 minutes;

      - Long Break: Set from 5 to 30 minutes;

      Just remember: You can't pause!
